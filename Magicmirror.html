Magic Mirror

The diminutive ruler, desperate to become king, needs to marry a princess, and the Magic Mirror is his high-tech (for the medieval setting, at least) solution. The Mirror itself is depicted as a framed object, almost like a television screen displaying images and information.The Magic Mirror is the same mirror seen in Snow White and the Seven Dwarfs. As a mirror, he has no identifiable gender, but his masculine voice leads viewers to see him as male. Mirror has a fairly minor role but appears in most of the Shrek films to date.
