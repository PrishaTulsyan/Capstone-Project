Donkey

Donkey is arguably the second main character of the Shrek franchise. He’s a talking donkey who even manages to procreate with a dragoness. He’s the greatest best friend Shrek could have ever hoped for.Donkey is a lively, talkative, and humorous character in the movie "Shrek." Serving as Shrek’s loyal sidekick and friend, Donkey brings a lighthearted and energetic dynamic to the story. Known for his endless chatter and optimistic attitude, Donkey often provides comic relief, balancing Shrek's gruff and sometimes solitary nature. Despite his talkativeness, Donkey is fiercely loyal, caring deeply for Shrek and playing a crucial role in helping him overcome challenges. His friendly and trusting nature helps build bridges between Shrek and others, making him an essential part of the team. Donkey’s blend of humor, warmth, and courage endears him to audiences and makes him one of the most memorable and beloved characters in the "Shrek" franchise.
